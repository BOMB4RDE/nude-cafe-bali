Our Story

Born from the belief that food should be honest.

We opened our doors in Berawa back in 2016 with one simple idea: create a space where people feel genuinely at home. No pretence, no theatre — just good food, good coffee, and the kind of warmth that makes you want to linger.

What started as an intimate spot for friends grew into something we never could have planned — a real community. Locals, expats, nomads, travellers. All finding the same thing at the same table: a place that feels real in a world that often doesn't.

Today we have four homes across Bali. The menu has evolved, the team has grown, and the spaces have changed — but the spirit has never wavered. We are, and always will be, nüde.

What we stand for

Three things we refuse to compromise on.

01

Honest Food

Every dish is prepared with care and served without compromise. Real ingredients, real flavour. Nothing hidden — nothing performed.

02

Real Community

We are nothing without the people who walk through our doors. Locals and visitors alike — everyone belongs at the nüde table.

03

Bali Born

Rooted in the warmth and spirit of Bali. Our island shapes how we cook, how we host, and how we see the world around us.
