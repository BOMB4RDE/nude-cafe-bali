Our Story

A café that never
had anything to hide

Nüde started with one café in Berawa and a straightforward idea: cook real food for real people, make it honest, and the rest will follow. No gimmicks, no pretense — just quality ingredients, good coffee, and a space where everyone belongs.

"We named it Nüde because we had nothing to hide — and we never will."

Eight years later, four locations across Bali keep that same spirit alive. Families, surfers, digital nomads, locals who've been coming since day one — everyone welcome, always.
